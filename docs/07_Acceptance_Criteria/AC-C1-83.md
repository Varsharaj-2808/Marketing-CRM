# Acceptance Criteria: C1-83

**Source:** C1-83 - [STORY-4.2.1] As a Marketing Executive, I want to see today's and overdue follow-ups so that no opportunity is missed....
**Issue Type:** Story

## Criteria

Given a lead's Next Follow-up Date equals today's date, then it appears in the user's 'Follow-ups Today' queue from 12:00 AM that day.

Given a Next Follow-up Date has passed without a new follow-up being logged, then the lead is flagged 'Overdue' in red on both the Lead List and the owner's dashboard until a new follow-up is logged or the lead is closed.

Given a follow-up is overdue by 3 or more calendar days, then it additionally surfaces on the Admin Dashboard's 'At Risk' widget, listing the lead, owner, and days overdue.
